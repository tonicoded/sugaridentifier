Privacy Policy

We keep this simple: we only use data to make the app work.

Last updated: December 29, 2025 Contact: anthonyvvza@gmail.com

1. What this app does

  • You scan a barcode.
  • We try to find nutrition + ingredients from a public food database.
  • We use AI to spot hidden sugar words in the ingredients.

2. What we collect

  • Barcode you scan (the numbers).
  • Product info we fetch (name, sugar per 100g, serving size, ingredients text, if available).
  • AI request data we send for analysis (product name + ingredients text + sugar numbers).
  • Purchase info (if you buy Pro): subscription/entitlement status. Payments are handled by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase status.
  • Basic app info (like errors) so we can fix bugs.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for your name, address, or phone number.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not track you across other apps.

4. Where your data goes

  • On your device: your scan history is stored locally on your phone (so you can see it again).
  • Open Food Facts: we look up products by barcode using the Open Food Facts API (public database).
  • Supabase (Edge Function): the app sends the barcode + product fields to our server endpoint to run the AI step.
  • OpenAI: the Supabase function sends the ingredients text to OpenAI to generate a simple JSON analysis.
  • Apple / RevenueCat: purchase status is checked using Apple’s systems and RevenueCat.

5. How we use data

  • Show sugar numbers (per 100g and per serving when available).
  • Highlight hidden sugar words and sweeteners in ingredients.
  • Keep your scan history on-device.
  • Run subscriptions (Pro).
  • Fix bugs and improve reliability.

6. Data control (delete / reset)

  • In the app, you can use Delete account to clear local app data on this device.
  • This removes scan history and resets onboarding on your phone.

7. Security

  • We use HTTPS (encryption in transit) for network calls.
  • No system is perfect — keep your device secure and up to date.

8. Children

  • This app is not made for kids under 13.
  • If you think a child sent data, email us and we’ll help.

9. Changes

  • If we change this policy, we’ll update the date above.
